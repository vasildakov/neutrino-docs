# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-03-05

This Privacy Policy explains how **Neutrino** (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use our website and services.

## 1. What data we collect

### 1.1 Account and authentication data
If you sign in using a third-party provider (e.g. Google, X/Twitter, Facebook/Meta), we may receive and store:

- Provider user identifier (e.g. Google user ID, Facebook user ID)
- Name (if provided by the provider)
- Email address (if provided and permitted)
- Profile image URL (if provided)

We use this data to create or link your Neutrino account and to authenticate you.

### 1.2 Technical and usage data
When you access Neutrino, we may process:

- IP address (or a truncated/hashed form depending on configuration)
- Browser and device information (user agent)
- Pages viewed, timestamps, and basic interaction metrics
- Performance metrics (e.g. request duration)

This data is used for security, troubleshooting, and improving the service.

## 2. How we use your data
We use personal data to:

- Authenticate you and manage your account
- Provide and operate the service
- Improve reliability, performance, and user experience
- Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
- Comply with legal obligations (where applicable)

## 3. Legal basis (where applicable)
Depending on your context, we process data based on:

- Performance of a contract (providing the service)
- Legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, service improvement)
- Consent (where required for optional features)

## 4. Sharing and disclosure
We do not sell your personal data.

We may share data only:

- With authentication providers strictly as part of the login flow
- With service providers that help us operate the service (hosting, monitoring), under appropriate safeguards
- If required by law or to protect our rights and users

## 5. Data retention
We retain personal data only as long as needed for:

- Account operation and authentication
- Security and troubleshooting
- Legal or compliance requirements

You can request deletion at any time (see section 8).

## 6. Security
We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, including access controls and encryption where appropriate.

## 7. Children
Neutrino is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 8. Your rights and deletion requests
You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data.
